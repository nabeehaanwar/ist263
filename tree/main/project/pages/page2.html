Open When... It's a Rainy Day

  • Movie to watch: Knives Out
  • Song to listen to: Lover - Taylor Swift
  • Yummy snack to eat: Trader Joe's Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups

Might be a hot take, but rainy days in college can be some of the best days. Sometimes, it can be so relaxing and set the perfect scene for studying, so I would definitely light a candle, find a place near a big window, and enjoy the ambience of the drizzling against your window. And what better thing is there to do besides sit back and watch a movie? Rainy days were made for movies and especially for murder mysteries. Whatever you do, don’t let the gloomy weather get you down, instead, use it to your advantage!

Open When... You're missing home

  • Movie to watch: Big Hero 6
  • Song to listen to: What Part of Forever - Ceelo Green
  • Yummy snack to eat: Kraft Mac n Cheese

We all have those moments of missing home in the first few weeks of the semester, and it always sucks. Take this time to retreat back into your younger self, do everything the younger you always said you would and live out your best life in college! Don’t hesitate to call or video chat your parents, your hometown friends, or a loved one or even write them a couple of ‘Open When’ letters to spread a little bit of that happiness in your life. No matter what, make sure you give yourself time to deal with that feeling, have a self-care day, indulge in some of your favorite foods, put on a couple of face masks, pursue some new hobbies, and keep yourself going.

Open When... You need a night in with your best friend(s)

  • Movie to watch: Booksmart
  • Song to listen to: Best Friend - Saweetie, Doja Cat
  • Yummy snack to eat: Ben n Jerry’s Half-Baked Ice Cream

You finally finished all your assignments for the week and it’s finally time to hang out with your best friends! There is nothing better than finally getting the chance to de-stress and catch up on some bonding time with your favorite people. A good night in with some of your closest friends, matched with some ice cream, a great playlist, and hilarious late night coming of age flick is the best way to celebrate all the hard work you’ve been doing so far. Feel free to continue the festivities by going out and exploring your college town, trying new foods, and participating in some good old retail therapy. The limits are endless!

Open When... You need to unwind after class

  • Movie to watch: Crazy Rich Asians
  • Song to listen to: Time - Childish Gambino, Ariana Grande
  • Yummy snack to eat: Potato Chips

A long day in college often feels never-ending, so it’s important to take a good chunk of the day to yourself so you don’t burn yourself out. Make sure to remind yourself that it’s okay to take a break once in a while, and maybe a nice long nap to accompany it. A nice, feel good romance and some uplifting music is bound to refresh you and set you back on track to continue pursuing your dreams and hustle your way into an amazing career. You got this! Relax.

Open When... You need a good laugh

  • Movie to watch: Deadpool
  • Song to listen to: Mariposa - The Peach Tree Rascals
  • Yummy snack to eat: Chipotle Burrito

We all need a good laugh sometimes to keep us going, and watching a great comedy is perfect for doing just that. It’s so important to prioritize your happiness and every little boost of serotonin you can get through out the day. Remember not to take yourself so seriously all the time, let yourself let loose, and to try indulging in some of your favorite uplifting things.

Open When... You need motivation

  • Movie to watch: Good Will Hunting
  • Song to listen to: The Middle - Jimmy Eat World
  • Yummy snack to eat: Chickpea salad

I’m not sure what’s stumping you right now, but just know you’re way too good for it to be bothering you. You can face any challenge that comes your way! Here are some questions to ask yourself when you’re feeling stuck: Is this worth my time? Will I remember this in 5 years? Is there anything I can do to solve this problem? Try not to let yourself stress over things you don’t have too much control of and try to keep yourself healthy and refreshed. Kick back with a delicious and healthy chickpea salad, stay hydrated, and remember to always believe in yourself! Things will always have a way of working themselves out.
